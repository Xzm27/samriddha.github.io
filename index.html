I am currently working as a Data Scientist at the TATA Digital Ltd., the youngest brainchild of the of the TATA Group, working on multiple real-world problems in Data Science and Computer Vision since May '21. My base location is Mumbai and currently I am working from home, although I am planning to relocate to Mumbai soon.
